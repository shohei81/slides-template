左カラム

  • ポイント A
  • ポイント B

右カラム

  • 反対意見 A
  • 反対意見 B

Two-column layout

Left column

Use .twocol to split content into two equal columns. Good for compare-and-contrast.

  • Point A
  • Point B

Right column

Each child of .twocol becomes a column. Columns wrap independently.

  • Counterpoint A
  • Counterpoint B
「before / after」「モデル / データ」など、二項の対比に向くレイアウト。
Two-column slides are great for "before vs after," "model vs data," or any binary contrast.

Equations and tables KEY

Display math goes in .eq-block:

\[ p(h \mid X) \;\propto\; \underbrace{p(X \mid h)}_{\text{likelihood}}\; \underbrace{p(h)}_{\text{prior}} \]

KaTeX renders both inline \(...\) and display \[...\] math. The .label pill (KEY) is for tagging the central slides of the talk.

Figures with the white card

Drop your figure into figs/ and reference it from .fig-card

Replace figs/sample.svg with your figure. The white card frames dark figures cleanly.
Tip: PNG/SVG both work. For research figures pulled from papers, cite them in the caption.

The white-card framing makes figures pop against the dark theme. Keep the max-height around 500-600px so captions still fit.
